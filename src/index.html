ABOUT US

American Nursing College

KLD Road, Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh

The American Nursing College (ANC), located on KLD Road in Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh, is an esteemed institution recognized by the Andhra Pradesh Nursing and Midwifery Council (APNMC). Affiliated with YSR University (YSR), it stands as a premier health and educational academy in the region. ANC is dedicated to training proficient nursing professionals, contributing significantly to society both locally and globally. Embracing modern educational practices, ANC provides technologically-driven learning experiences, ensuring the highest standards in healthcare education. By adopting innovative approaches, ANC also plays a role in addressing the nation's unemployment challenges.READ MORE

OUR COURSES

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing)

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing) program aims to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of nursing theory and practice while developing critical thinking, leadership, and communication skills. Emphasizing evidence-based care and cultural competence, the program prepares graduates to deliver high-quality patient care across diverse healthcare settings. With a focus on ethical practice and lifelong learning, BSc Nursing graduates are poised to address evolving healthcare needs, advocate for patients' rights, and contribute positively to the healthcare system.

Learn More

General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

The purpose of GNM nursing courses is to equip students with essential skills needed for diverse healthcare roles, including patient care, medication administration, and medical equipment management. Through practical training and theoretical knowledge, GNM programs prepare individuals to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare sector, ensuring quality care delivery and contributing positively to societal well-being.
